Features

⏳ Temproles

The number of temporary roles that you can give to your members.

👋 Joinroles

The number of temproles that you can set as join roles to let your new members get it when joining your server for the first time.

🔑 Licenses

The number of License Keys that you can create to let your members redeem them and get temprole.

🅱 Buttons

The number of buttons that you can create to let your members get temprole by clicking on it.

✅ Reactions

The number of reactions that you can create to let your members get temprole by clicking on it.

🔁 Recovery

Let your members take the remaining duration of their temporary roles when they rejoin your server.

👔 Staff

The number of members/roles that you can allow to use mod/admin commands.

💬 Notifications

Send a direct message to the members when add/remove/edit/expire temprole.

⏰ Reminder

Send a direct message to members a few days before the temprole expires.

🔌 Integrations

The number of bots/webhooks that will be accepted to use text-commands.

🚀 Boosts

Reward your members when boosting your server by giving them a temprole.
